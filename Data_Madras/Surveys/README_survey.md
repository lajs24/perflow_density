The surveys were conducted in the field by 6 staff members, around 11pm on Friday, December 9, 2022.
Passers-by close to the entrance of Place des Terreaux were asked the following questions:
1) `How many people were with you?'
2) `How many children were with you?'
3) `What was the last screening you attended before the one at Place des Terreaux?'
4) `What screening do you plan to attend next? (You can answer that you don't know)'

The CSV file contains one line per answer collected on the field, with the following columns:
* [Adults]: stated number of adults in the social group
* [Children]:  stated number of children in the social group
* [total] = [Adults] + [Children]
* From: answer to question 3) about the origin
* To: answer to question 4) about the next destination
* From_keyword: classification of origins made by the coders
* To_keyword: classification of destinations made by the coders

"DNK" stands for "does not know"
